## Best Massage Gun 2026
**Target question:** "What is the best massage gun for home use in 2026?"
**Quora topic:** Massage Therapy / Fitness Recovery / Percussive Therapy
**URL to link:** https://pickly.blog/en/articles/best-massage-gun-2026/

---
Depends heavily on how you actually train. I went through five percussive guns — from a 9,800-yen Bob and Brad C2 to a 79,900-yen Theragun PRO Plus — and the biggest surprise was how much stroke amplitude matters versus what brands advertise.

**Theragun PRO Plus (grade A+)** — Best for serious athletes. 16 mm stroke amplitude and 27 kg stall force means it actually reaches deep muscle rather than skating across the surface. The triangular grip genuinely helps when treating your own upper back. Weight is 1.4 kg, which becomes noticeable overhead after 5 minutes.

**Best for budget:** Bob and Brad C2 — At one-eighth the price, it handles shoulder, calf, and thigh work adequately. The 10 mm stroke is too shallow for dense back muscle but covers 80% of what casual users need.

The main thing to consider: stroke amplitude, not rpm. Any gun advertising high rpm without showing amplitude is hiding a shallow-stroke weakness. Under 12 mm and you're basically using a vibrator on your muscles, not a percussion tool.

Also worth knowing before you buy — there are specific body areas where percussion causes actual harm (spine, joints, kidneys, carotid area). The full list is in my comparison.
